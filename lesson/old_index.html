Introduction of the lesson

We provide 3 kinds of lesson.

Group Lesson

It’s a basic class of the PILATES.
You could learn about the thoracic breathing, moving pelvic, and right posture which should be mastered in order to continue PILATES.
You could also learn about the essential point of the PILATES and how to use your body more naturally.

» Guidance and Fees

Semi-Private Lesson

You could learn about the PILATES with your friend or colleague adjusting to your purpose and your schedule.  You could concentrate on the lesson without any worry.  And it’s fit for the dancer and athlete who would like to improve the specific function of the body

» Guidance and Fees

Private Lesson

This is one on one lesson focusing on your individual needs.
From the basic to the advanced one, you could learn about the PILATES one on one.
It’s best fit for the person who want to concentrate to the training and also for those who care for more privacy (e.g. pregnant women, elderly people, etc.)
