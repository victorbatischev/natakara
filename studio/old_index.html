From Station

The studio is 2min. walk from the Denentoshi Line "Sangenjaya" station.
Exit A and walk toward Shibuya. Turn to the right at the second signal. You can see the signboard on the left soon.
The studio is 5min. walk from the Setagaya Line "Sangenjaya" station.
Go through the street of Setagaya Post Office and cross the root 246 in front of the Koban. Walk to Shibuya and turn to the right at the second signal. You can see the signboard on the left soon.

Parking note

The studio do not have a parking space or its own. You may use a nearby “Coin Parking” if you wish to.
