What I should expect in typical PILATES lesson in your studio?
First of all, we will help you introducing to yourself.  We start with breathing, warm up exercise including stretching and leading into the fundamental lessons of PILATES. Finally closing the lesson with deep relaxation.

To pay maximum attention to each participant, we are restricting number of proper lesson as below.

  • Group lesson – Max. 6 people per lesson
  • Semi private lesson – 2 people per lesson

What is the PILATES breathing?

PILATES Breathing is the foundation of your health, beauty and youth.  
When you do take a deep inhalation, it energies your body just like a newly born.
Your ribs become open, your lungs are saturated with oxygen.  It this moment, muscles of your abdominal(belly) are working, too.  Oxygen enters into the blood stream and purifies the blood.  When you do maximum exhalation it starts to remove your stress, pain, sadness and any bitterness from the past, gradually.  Pilates breathing ventilates your lungs, saturates your blood with oxygen. It sharpens your brain with improved memory resulting in increased efficiency.  In my experience, I can say that many people find the difficulty in breathing freely.  If people can not breath properly, they deprive themselves of life.  After realizing the importance of proper breathing, you can manifest your dreams, desires, in real life.

About relaxation, what is it?

After an hour of productive PILATES session, your body, brain and consciousness needs rest.  in your self and body, your brain and our consciousness needs rest.  I have introduced relaxation in my lessons and I have seen it’s positive effects at various levels including the body, mind and also on subconscious.  In relaxation, human brain is calmed down, body gains strength, consciousness become crystal clear.  Introduction to your self in a new way is interesting and important experience for all of us. While relaxing we pay attention to every part of our body and fill it with rejoice or happiness, since our body has served us through series of exercises.   The same process occurs in the relaxation.   When we relax our body from head to toe, we begin to feel our body, its needs and wishes.
Relaxation is a kind of pleasure rejuvenation.  Practicing PILATES with relaxation continuously 3 times per week, you will fell like under 10-years old.
